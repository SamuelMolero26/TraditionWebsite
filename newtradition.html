New Tradition: Sunset at Zachary Engineering Complex

Date:
First Sunday of every semester, right before the start of classes.
Time:
5:30 PM to catch the sunset around 6:00 PM.
Location:
Zachary Engineering Education Complex
Participants:
Open to all engineering students, alumni, and faculty

Purpose:
Give students a chance to relax alongside their peers, and reflect before the semester begins. Symbolizing the closing of one chapter and the start of a new one in their academic journey. Building a sense of community and support. More than just a simple tradition, the idea is t bring together students, giving them an opportunity to connect and develop friendships.
